🔄 Drive Mode

LB (Left Bumper) HOLD Robot-Centric Mode — Switches from field-centric to robot-centric while held.

🎯 Action Buttons

ButtonTypeAction
A HOLD Snap to Nearest 45° — Rotates the robot to the nearest 45° heading increment and holds it.
Y HOLD Prepare To Fire — Aims at hub using Limelight TX with PD control, reads distance. Drivetrain rotates toward hub while driver retains translation.
B TOGGLE Aim-At-Hub While Driving — Press to activate, press again to deactivate. Robot auto-rotates to face hub while driver retains full translation control. Operator can fire while driver repositions.

Unassigned on Driver: X, RB, DPad, Start, Back.

Tip: Use Driver Y (PrepareToFire) to aim, then Operator RT to fire.
